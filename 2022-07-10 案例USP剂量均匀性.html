案例二




Q: If uncoated tablet contains 2 drug substances but only one of them meets the requirement for WV, how can the requirement be met?


如果未包衣片剂含有两种原料药，但其中只有一种符合 WV 的要求，如何才能满足要求


A: In the case of a two-component tablet, uniformity of dosage units test requirement will be met by the WV for the component >25 mg of drug substance comprising >25% of the weight of the dosage unit mass. The other component will require the CU.


在双组分片剂的情况下，剂量单位测试要求的均一性大于 25 mg 的成分（占剂量单位质量重量的 大于25%） 满足WV 要求，另一个成分满足CU要求
