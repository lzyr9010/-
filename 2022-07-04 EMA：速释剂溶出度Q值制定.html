EMA有一篇Q值的制定指南：Reflection paper on the dissolution specification for generic solid oral immediate release products with systemic action



关键词：BE批，n=12，Q值在75%-85%，5%间隔（也就是75%、80%、85%）


If dissolution of the biobatch is larger than or equal to 95% in 15 minutes, the specification may be set to Q=85% after 15 minutes

BE批15min溶出（n=12）≥95%，15min溶出的Q值=85%

If dissolution of the biobatch is less than 95% but larger than or equal to 85% in 15 minutes, the specification (Q) may be set to 75%, 80% or 85% whichever is closer to Q=biobatch result -10% at 15 minutes

85%≤BE批15min溶出（n=12）<95%，15min溶出的Q值可设置75%、80%、85%，以更接近生物批溶出-10%为准

如BE批15min溶出（n=12）为93%，93%-10%=83%，Q值为85%

If dissolution of the biobatch is larger than or equal to 85% only after 30 minutes, the specification (Q) may be set to 75%, 80% or 85% whichever is closer to Q=biobatch result -10% at 30 minutes

BE批30min溶出（n=12）≥85%，30min溶出的Q值可设置75%、80%、85%，以更接近生物批溶出-10%为准

BE批30min溶出（n=12）为88%，88%-10%=78%，Q值为80%

If dissolution of the biobatch is larger than or equal to 85% only after 45 minutes, the specification may be set to 75%, 80% or 85% after 45 minutes

BE批45min溶出（n=12）≥85%，45min溶出的Q值可设置75%、80%、85%，以更接近生物批溶出-10%为准

In case dissolution of the biobatch is less than or equal to 85% after 45 minutes, a minimum of 75% at 45 minutes should be specified if possible. Otherwise, if the dissolution specification (Q) is less than 75% after 45 minutes, the dissolution specification should be based on more than one time point (see Annex: Decision tree for the principles for setting specifications)


75%≤BE批45min溶出（n=12）<85%，45min溶出的Q值=75%。如果上述均不满足，溶出度标准应多于一个时间点
